Adding a New Section

When adding a new IPC section, consider the following:

  • Section Code: Use the official IPC section number. For subsections, append letters (e.g., 304A).
  • Section Name: Use the official name of the section as it appears in the IPC.
  • Description: Provide a clear and concise description of what the section covers, including key elements of the offense.
Adding new sections will affect the AI's ability to map complaints to legal sections. After adding new sections, consider retraining the ML model.
